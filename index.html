Who We Are

Rooted in the
Highlands of India

IPES International is a premium tropical produce exporting firm headquartered in Ambalavayal, with sourcing operations spanning Madikeri and Kodaikanal — three of India's most fertile and pristine growing regions.

We connect conscientious global buyers with the world-class tropical produce that flourishes in the unique microclimates of the Western Ghats and Palani Hills. Every shipment carries the integrity of the land it was grown on.
